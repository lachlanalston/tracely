# NBN NTD/NTU LED Status Guide

This guide covers LED indicators for different NBN connection types and common troubleshooting tips.

---

## 1. Fibre to the Premises (FTTP)

| LED   | Color / Status   | Meaning                           | Troubleshooting         |
| ----- | ---------------- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Power | Green (Solid)    | Device is powered on              | Check power if off      |
| Link  | Blue (Solid)     | Active internet connection        | Check Ethernet cable    |
| LAN   | Green (Solid)    | Ethernet connection active        | Check cable connections |
| Wi-Fi | Green (Flashing) | Wi-Fi activity                    | Ensure Wi-Fi is enabled |
| Phone | Green (Solid)    | Phone line active (if applicable) | Check phone setup       |

**General Troubleshooting Tips:**

* Power Cycle: Turn off the device, wait 30 seconds, and turn it back on.
* Check Connections: Ensure all cables are securely connected.
* Contact Support: If issues persist, contact your internet service provider.
